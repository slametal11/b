Double Chicken Please

Double Chicken Please or DCP is a bar on the Lower East Side of Manhattan in New York City. The bar was established in 2020 and was ranked as the 2nd best bar in the world on 2023's The World's 50 Best Bars. The bar consists of two sections; The Front Room which serves cocktails on tap known as draft cocktails which is walk-in only; and The Coop which serves craft cocktails inspired by food such as Japanese cold soba, key lime pie, and mango sticky rice along with riffs on classic cocktails. Along with said cocktails, the restaurant serves Taiwanese inspired fried chicken sandwiches such as with salted egg yolk or with a mochi crust along with a few other offerings.
